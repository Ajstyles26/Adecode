I am pleased to inform you that you have been offered provisional admission to AJAYI CROWTHER UNIVERSITY, OYO to pursue B.A Christian Religious Studies with details as follows:

  6. You are required to present to the University at the time of Screening/Registration, Letter of Reference from a Christian Clergy, and a person of reputable standing in the society vouching for your good behavior.
